02 — About 12

Design that
holds its ground.

Twelve years building visual languages for brands that want to last. I work across identity, type, editorial and interface — not because I can't specialise, but because the best work lives in the space between disciplines.

Before going independent I led design at Bureau Grotesque in Copenhagen and was senior art director at The Gourmand in London. I hold an MA in Communication Design from the RCA.

What I do

Capabilities

How I work

The process

Every engagement begins with a listening phase. Two to four hours of structured conversation to understand the business, its audience, its competitive context and the gap between where it is and where it wants to be.
A written positioning document delivered before any visual work begins. This becomes the brief, the filter and the north star for every decision that follows.
One considered direction, not three half-baked ones. I present fully realised work at each stage with a clear rationale, then refine in two focused rounds of feedback.
Comprehensive brand guidelines, all production files in open formats and a recorded walkthrough so your team can operate the system independently from day one.
